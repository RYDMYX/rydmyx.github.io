About RYDMYX

RYDMYX is a platform designed to develop tools for music and structured learning. The goal is to create functional, precise, and practical systems that support learning, experimentation, and skill development.

The platform currently includes tools such as:

  • Tules – a tool for musical and structured exploration.
  • Tunstek – a system focused on tuning and audio-based interaction.

This project is independently developed and is intended to grow into a set of educational and creative tools.
